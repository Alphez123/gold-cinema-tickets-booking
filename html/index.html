About Us

Our Story

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We're open from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM Monday to Thursday, and 10:00 AM to 1:00 AM Friday to Sunday.

Do you offer refunds?

Tickets can be refunded up to 2 hours before the showtime. After that, exchanges may be possible depending on availability.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have complimentary parking for all our customers with over 500 spaces available.

Can I bring outside food?

For hygiene and safety reasons, we don't allow outside food or beverages in our theaters.

Do you have wheelchair access?

Yes, all our theaters are fully accessible with wheelchair spaces and companion seating available.

How early should I arrive?

We recommend arriving at least 20 minutes before the showtime to find parking, collect tickets, and get snacks.
